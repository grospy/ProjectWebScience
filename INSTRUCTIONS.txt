1.	Launch application

2.	Press Data -> Load Data, if you have the Data files

	[Skip to step 6, if you have the data]

3.	Press Crawler -> Start Crawling, to collect data with the webcrawler

4.	Let the Crawler work for a specific period of time (longer crawling time = more accurate suggestions) 

5.	Press Crawler -> Stop Crawling, to stop the webcrawler

6.	Input the restaurant's ID in the text field [Restaurant IDs: 44, 24339, 33599, 37546]

7.	Press one of the buttons, depending on your interest

8.	Receive the suggestions
